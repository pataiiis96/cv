Haroon Ahmads Portfolio Page

CEO of Eczemadoc

Haroon is the Co-Founder and CEO of Eczemadoc - A digital health app empowering patients to seamlessly track, understand and ultimately manage their eczema. He also works as an Academic Foundation Doctor in Leadership at the Royal Bolton Hospital and has been recognised as one of the countries youngest NHS Clinical Entrepreneurs.

Haroon graduated from the University of Birmingham and completed a Management BSc at Imperial College London. He has gained industry experience as a consultant for a venture backed healthcare company and held roles within the European Institute of Technology and Innovation as a regional lead.

Haroon is a passionate digital health advocate and is on a mission to create a paradigm shift in the way skin conditions are treated, by developing the next generation dermatology clinic.
